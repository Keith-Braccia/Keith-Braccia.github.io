Privacy Policy for Renewal

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Renewal ("we," "us," or "our") 
handles information when you use the Renewal mobile application 
(the "App").

THE SHORT VERSION

Renewal is designed to work locally on your device. Your home 
maintenance schedules, subscription records, and appliance logs 
are stored on your device, not on our servers. We do not sell 
your data. We do not show ads. We do not track your behavior 
for marketing purposes.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information you provide
When you use Renewal, you create records for home maintenance 
tasks, subscriptions, appliance warranties, and shared 
checklists. This information is stored locally on your device 
using on-device storage (SQLite). It does not leave your device 
unless you explicitly enable cloud sync or family sharing 
features.

Photos and attachments
If you use the Home Log feature to attach photos or receipts to 
an appliance record, those photos are stored locally on your 
device. If you enable cloud sync, they are uploaded to secure 
cloud storage solely to make them available across your devices.

Optional account and sync
Core Renewal features work without an account. If you choose to 
create an account to enable sync across devices or family 
sharing, we collect your email address and use it solely to 
authenticate your account. We do not use it for marketing 
without your explicit consent.

Usage information
We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry 
by default. If we add optional crash reporting in a future 
version, we will update this policy and ask for your consent 
before enabling it.

HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the information stored in the App solely to provide the 
App's features to you. Specifically:

- Your maintenance schedules are used to send you local 
  notifications when items are due.
- Your subscription records are used to calculate costs and 
  flag unused subscriptions.
- Your appliance records and photos are used to display your 
  Home Log.
- If sync is enabled, your data is transmitted to our servers 
  solely to make it available on your other devices.

We do not use your data to build advertising profiles, sell to 
third parties, or train machine learning models.

DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with 
third parties for their marketing purposes.

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

- With household members you explicitly invite, and only the 
  specific checklists or shared items you choose to share with 
  them.
- With Apple or Google for the purpose of processing in-app 
  purchases, as required by their platforms.
- If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our 
  users.

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted 
on-device storage. If you enable cloud sync, your data is 
transmitted over HTTPS and stored in encrypted cloud storage. 
We retain synced data for as long as your account is active. 
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time 
from the App's settings.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Renewal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do 
not knowingly collect personal information from children under 
13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such 
information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your 
personal data, including the right to access, correct, or 
delete it. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address 
below. Because most of your data is stored locally on your 
device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App 
or clearing App data in your device settings.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will 
notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the 
App or updating the "last updated" date above. Continued use of 
the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated 
policy.
