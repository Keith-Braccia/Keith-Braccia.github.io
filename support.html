Frequently asked questions

Notifications aren't firing.
Make sure notifications are enabled for Renewal in your device Settings. On iOS: Settings → Renewal → Notifications → Allow Notifications.

I lost my data after reinstalling.
Renewal stores data locally on your device. Enable iCloud backup in your device settings to prevent data loss on reinstall.
